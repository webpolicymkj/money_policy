Privacy Policy for Personal Money Tracker


1. Introduction
Your privacy is important to us. This Privacy Policy explains how the Personal Money Tracker app ("the App") collects, uses, shares, and protects your information in compliance with applicable privacy laws (e.g., GDPR, CCPA) and Google Play policies.
By installing and using the App, you agree to the terms outlined in this policy.


2. Your Financial Data Stays on Your Device
Personal Money Tracker is an offline-first personal finance app. All of the financial information you enter — including transactions, income, expenses, budgets, categories, selected currency, and language preferences — is stored locally on your device only.
We do NOT upload, transmit, or store your transactions or budget data on our servers. We cannot read your financial records, and they are never sold or shared.


3. Information Collected by Third-Party Services
The App uses the Internet permission only to display ads and to collect basic, anonymous analytics that help us keep the App running and improve it. Through third-party SDKs, the following limited data may be collected automatically:

Device Info: Device model, operating system version, language

Advertising ID (GAID): Used by advertising and analytics services

App Usage & Diagnostics: Features used, app open frequency, and crash/performance logs

IP Address: Processed by advertising and analytics services

We do not collect your name, email, contacts, photos, location, or files.


4. Why This Data Is Collected
Show ads to support the free App (AdMob)

Measure app usage and improve features

Detect, diagnose, and fix crashes and bugs


We do not sell personal data or use it for automated profiling with legal effects.


5. Third-Party Services
The App integrates the following third-party services, each with its own privacy policy:

Google AdMob – advertising (https://policies.google.com/privacy)

Google Firebase – analytics, crash reporting, and remote configuration (https://firebase.google.com/support/privacy)

Facebook (Meta) SDK – advertising and analytics (https://www.facebook.com/policy.php)

AppsFlyer – attribution and analytics (https://www.appsflyer.com/legal/services-privacy-policy/)

These partners comply with Google Play's data policies. We do not sell your data to third parties.


6. Permissions Used
INTERNET: Required to load ads and send anonymous analytics/crash reports. The App does not request location, camera, microphone, contacts, or storage management permissions.


7. Advertising
The App displays ads provided by third-party ad networks. These networks may use the Advertising ID to show personalized or non-personalized ads. Where required by law, the App shows a consent form (Google UMP) so you can choose your ad preferences. You can reset or opt out of personalized ads at any time in your device's ad settings.


8. Your Rights (GDPR, CCPA)
Depending on your region, you have the right to:

Access, update, or delete data associated with you

Withdraw consent or revoke app permissions

Opt out of personalized advertising

Request information about the categories of data collected and shared

Because your financial data is stored only on your device, you can delete it at any time by clearing it inside the App or uninstalling the App.


9. Data Retention
Your on-device financial data remains until you delete it or uninstall the App.
Analytics and crash data collected by third-party services are typically retained for no longer than necessary for the purposes described above and in accordance with each provider's policy.


10. Children's Privacy
The App is not intended for children under 13. We do not knowingly collect personal data from children.


11. Data Transfers
Data processed by third-party services may be stored or processed on servers outside your country. These providers implement safeguards in line with GDPR and other applicable laws.


12. Changes to This Policy
We may update this Privacy Policy periodically. The latest version will always be available at this page, and the effective date will reflect the most recent update.


13. About This Policy
This Privacy Policy applies to the Personal Money Tracker app.
